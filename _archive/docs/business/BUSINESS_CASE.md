# TiranaFly: Business Case & Financial Analysis

## 1. CAPEX (Capital Expenditure)
*   **Infrastructure**: 5 Depots @ $150,000 each = $750,000.
*   **Fleet**: 100 Drones (TF-V1) @ $12,000 each = $1,200,000.
*   **Control Center**: IT Systems & GIS Infrastructure = $300,000.
*   **Total CAPEX**: **$2,250,000**.

## 2. OPEX (Operating Expenditure)
*   **Energy**: $0.05/km @ 2M km/year = $100,000.
*   **Maintenance**: $200,000/year.
*   **Staffing**: 10 Operators/Technicians = $400,000/year.
*   **Total OPEX**: **$700,000/year**.

## 3. Business Impact
*   **Cost per Delivery**: Reduced from $8.50 (Ground) to $2.10 (Air).
*   **Carbon Offset**: 450 Tons CO2 reduction annually.
*   **Service Speed**: 75% reduction in delivery wait time in the Tirana core.

## 4. ROI Analysis
*   **Break-even Point**: 3.2 Years.
*   **Net Present Value (NPV)**: $4.5M over 7 years.
